Welcome to Imawanacode Island

Are you ready for the vacation of your life?
Here we have plenty of activities for the whole family. From scuba diving, to parasailing, to hiking and taking in some of the most breathtaking sights.

Scuba Diving Lessons

Whether a seasoned pro or a newbie we have several spots you have to see!

Kayaking

Through the rivers or out in the open rent a Kayak and see where it'll take you!

Windsurfing

Want a sport you can take at your own speed. Windsurfing is for you!
